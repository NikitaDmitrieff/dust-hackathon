USER INTENT ANALYSIS
====================

1. **User's Main Intent/Goal:**
   - The user wants to create a form to collect specific information about their friends, specifically regarding their hair characteristics, height, and age, in order to better understand them.

2. **Key Requests or Needs Mentioned:**
   - The user wants the form to be filled out directly by their friends.
   - They prefer using menus with predefined choices for ease of completion.
   - Specific fields include:
     - Hair: predefined choices for color and length.
     - Height: predefined ranges.
     - Age: predefined ranges.

3. **Specific Actions They Want Taken:**
   - The user wants the assistant to suggest a simple and easy-to-fill format for the form, with predefined options for each category to ensure it is user-friendly for their friends.
